About vavubali

On the occasion of the new moon of the month of Adi, the VLC on the banks of the Kulithura river was organized by the Kulithura municipality. It is held annually at the stadium.

History:

The Vavupali fair started in 1926. At that time, people would gather on both sides of the Kulithura river on the last new moon day of Adi month to offer sacrifices to their dead ancestors. This Vauvali exhibition was started in a way to attract the people who gathered.

Kulithura Vavupali Exhibition A 20-day Vavupali exhibition will be held on the ground owned by the Kulithura municipality on the banks of the river. It is customary for farmers to put their agricultural products on display for the public. Also, the exhibition is being conducted by various departments including the horticulture department. Apart from these, entertainment features including giant rattan, magic and various other side shows are featured here.
